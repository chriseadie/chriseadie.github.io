A small storage company called Family Box needed a website that would allow their customers to find out more information about what they do and to help them sign up a get a one of the storage packages that they offer. The website is targeted towards small business that need help keeping some of their more important documents off site.


The website needed to be accessible to a wide audience and so the ability for the website to be responsive was a large priority.The website was built using a W3 CSS framework which was then re-coded and changed to fit the needs of Family Box. All of the icons and graphics used throughout respond with the site so the users receive the same experience no matter which device they use.Family Box also required some graphic design work to be done.


I designed the logo for the business, they wanted to incorporate the idea of security into the logo and after some brain storming the idea was to put the combination lock for a vault into the logo to replace the O in box. This was to encompass the element of security into the logo.


Once they were happy with the logo this lead to designing business cards, letter heads and envelopes. With the use of the graphic branding this help make the company come across to to their clients as more professional.
