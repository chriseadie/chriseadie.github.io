V Base is a student volunteering centre located at Edinburgh Napier university. V Base have an issue where they would like to increase the number of students from the school of computing. V Base would also like to create a community out of volunteering at Edinburgh Napier and encourage students to come back and be apart of something else.Â 


V base wished to also come up with a concept for gamifying the experience as well through applying a points system based on opportunities that the students had completed. The idea of using a tinder based approach for students to select their opportunities was also conceived as a new a way of having student interact with the application.


For designing V Base a usable solution Human centred design method was used to approach the problem. this method allowed insight to be made from what students actually wanted from V Base and from their experience volunteering.
